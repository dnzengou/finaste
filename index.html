Dress code

“Dressy casual”

  • Some of you have asked what the dress code means, so here’s the main description: For ladies -try a cocktail dress or a dressy skirt and top. An elegant pantsuit is also appropriate. Floor-length gowns, however, might be too fancy. Pair with heels, nice wedges or elegant flats. For the men - a suit in a light color, with or without tie. But most important is that you feel comfortable and handsome/pretty.

Where

Directions

  • From Stenungsund Station you can take Bus nr 4 to Jordhammars Herrgård and Ödsmåls Kyrka (Jordhammar is the stop before Ödsmåls Kyrka). It only takes 9 min. The bus stops are just 50 m from the destinations, which both can easily be seen on your left side of the road. Of course, you can also take bus 4 back to Stenungsund if you like.
